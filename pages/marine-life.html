Marine Life — Visitor Basics

Stay within permitted areas and follow local guidance.

Common Species

Visitors may encounter fish, seabirds, and occasionally turtles in certain regions.

  • Keep respectful distance
  • Avoid touching or feeding

Viewing Basics

Use viewing platforms where available and follow local guidance.

  • Bring binoculars
  • Learn signage

Tide & Weather Notes

Conditions influence visibility and access to certain areas.

  • Check forecasts
  • Plan alternative activities

Responsible Conduct

Protect habitats by keeping to marked areas and reducing litter.

  • Pack out waste
  • Use designated bins

FAQs

Can I snorkel anywhere?

Only where permitted and conditions are safe. Follow local signage and advice.

Are there seasonal closures?

Some areas close to protect wildlife. Respect any restrictions.
