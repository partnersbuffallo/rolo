Snorkeling Guide — Simple How-To

Choose calm, permitted areas and never snorkel alone.

Preparation

Practice mask clearing in shallow water.

During Snorkel

Move slowly and avoid touching marine life.

After Snorkel

Rinse gear and secure it for next time.

FAQs

Do I need fins?

Fins can help but are optional in calm, shallow areas.

What if my mask fogs?

Use anti-fog products or a mild soap solution; practice clearing techniques.
