Australian Coasts — Overview

Plan around local conditions and amenities; choose routes suitable for your group.

East Coast

Surf beaches, boardwalks, and coastal towns. Plan parking and early starts in busy seasons.

  • Use patrolled areas where available
  • Check local conditions

Tropical North

Warm waters with seasonal considerations; heed local signage and guidance.

  • Protective gear where advised
  • Plan shade and hydration

West Coast

Long, dramatic stretches with quieter bays and marine parks.

  • Carry water
  • Check drive times and fuel

Southern Shores

Cooler waters, cliffs, and scenic lookouts with walking tracks.

  • Wear sturdy shoes
  • Respect barriers and signage

FAQs

When are beaches busiest?

Weekends, holidays, and warm seasons are popular. Early visits help with parking and space.

Is all swimming supervised?

No. Look for patrolled areas and follow local guidance.

Are coastal walks suitable for families?

Many are, with varying lengths and grades. Choose tracks that match your group.
